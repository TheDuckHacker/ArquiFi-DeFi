Impermanent loss is a potential risk for anyone providing liquidity to a decentralized exchange (DEX). It's the difference in value between holding tokens in a liquidity pool versus just holding them in your wallet. It's "impermanent" because the loss only becomes real if you withdraw your liquidity at that specific point in time.
